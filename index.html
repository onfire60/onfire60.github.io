CREATIVITY ONFIRE

Creative Portfolio of Designer, Developer Daniel Easly

Hello and welcome to OnFireDesigns.com. My name is Daniel Easly, and I am currently lead developer at Informatics Studio in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I have created this site to promote myself as a designer and developer as well as offer a resource for beginners and experts to expand their knowledge. My goal as a designer is to help clients develop aesthetically pleasing designs with their own uniquely "hot" style.

I offer a wide variety of services such as web design and development, corporate identity creation, and illustration. If you would like more information on the services I offer please browse my “Hire Me” page. I hope you enjoy my site!

Sincerely,
Daniel Easly
