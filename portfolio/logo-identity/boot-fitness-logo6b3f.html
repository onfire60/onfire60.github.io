Boot Fitness Logo

The Boot Fitness Logo was an early personal project I had done for a friend’s friend. The client was a Marine at the time and had the idea of bringing military style fitness to the masses. For this logo I was to bring the feel of hardcore military with a cartoon look to give it a friendlier feel and appeal to a broader audience.

The main focus of this logo was a cartoon illustration of the classic Marine boot stepping in the mud. This brought the feeling of being down, dirty and physical while still maintaining a fun nature. For the typeface I chose to stay with the aggressive military stamped serif font. This also maintained the feel of military boot camp.

Although this logo was successful, the future of this camp was bleak. Needless to say the logo didn't make it further than the design and website implementation.
