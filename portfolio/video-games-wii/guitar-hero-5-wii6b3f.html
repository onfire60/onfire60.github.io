Guitar Hero 5 Wii

This project took place at Vicarious Visions where I was contracted to do User Interface, UI work for Guitar Hero 5 for the Wii game system. My job was to create innovative graphics for the new and upcoming game. I was in charge of creating graphics for the game menu system as well as for the game play art itself.

I thoroughly enjoyed my time at Vicarious and was so ecstatic to be working on my first video game ever. I accomplished so much during this time, almost too much to explain here, but you can view all of the work I did for the game in the project gallery where I have screenshots and photos of my work. You can even see a video of my name in the credits of the game at the bottom of the gallery. It’s just amazing to think that my hand in art for a video game will last forever. It's an awesome feeling being an avid gamer myself to know that my name will last throughout the ages in this game.
