Lazio Construction Site Design

This was one of the first projects I completed at Overit Media. Lazio Construction is primarily a brick laying, construction company. They produce the usual masonry techniques as well as the ability to produce veneer brick masonry over existing facades.

I designed this site based off of the existing logo for Lazio Construction. Since they primarily do masonry, I chose to design the site like it was built from masonry bricks. The navigation was designed to be a side navigation differing from the traditional top navigation. I decided to add a small gallery to each page so they could show off their work regardless of the page the user is on. Color choice remained light as the logo for Lazio was of a dark nature.

Unfortunately it was decided at Overit that the site needed a more corporate look so it was redesigned. I felt that this didn't really give the client a unique look and feel to the site, but it is the corporate design world and the client’s decision. There are some things that I would change about the design, but I feel that my original had a unique spark.
