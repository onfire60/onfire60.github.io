Owl The Band Site

Link to the site: Owl The Band Site

This project was completed for the band of the President of Overit Media. The site design was to be based on a full flash website design. Designing for a flash site is different than most sites because you have to take into account all animations and flow of the site.

The main site design was to be based on illustrations done for Owl, giving a kind of grunge rock feel; dark and ominous. Each page was to incorporate these illustrations on the right and left side of the content. The font choice was a Gothic serif font that further pushed the feel of the site. I designed the site to feel like it had different sections that moved around vertically which also posed the challenge making all content dynamic.

The home page design was to include as much information about the site without being too overbearing. Being dynamic it was able to include new site releases, latest videos and photos.

The about section was to include text information based on the band as a whole and each individual band member. Each of the pages in this section included a large photo indicative of the subject matter on the left with a large scrolling box of text describing that subject.

The news section was designed to be totally dynamic giving the ability to add three images to each news story and obviously a text section. The right side lets the user select a specific news article and buttons that let you see latest news and the entire archive of news.

The events section was simple in that you dynamically add events, click on them and the event information was to display on the right.

The press section is exactly the same as the news section with the only difference being that you can add a video or audio clip to the article.

Because the media section was obviously one of the largest sections for the band, I decided to create audio and video player interfaces from scratch. Then I would create functioning players in flash. The photo viewer would be a 3D scrolling sideshow.

The store was a basic dynamic scrolling list of products for Owl the Band. Showing the description, price and image of the product along with a buy link to the store site. The links page was a designed to be a dynamic set of categorized links and the contact page was designed as a simple form in flash.

Overall, I feel the design works and really brings the intentions and style of the band into view.
