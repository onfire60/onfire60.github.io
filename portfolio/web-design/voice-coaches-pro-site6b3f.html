Voice Coaches Pro Site

Link to the Site: Voice Coaches Pro

This was a project completed for Voice Coaches, a client of Overit Media. The purpose of the project was to complete a design that extended a more professional version of voicecoahes.com for more experienced voice over clients. The design was to represent a similar look and feel to the main site.

Instead of the navigation of the original site, I decided to incorporate a horizontal top navigation. The color scheme was to be similar to the original but skewed just a bit to give the site a different look. Right underneath the navigation I added a photo slideshow to showoff either ads or imagery representative of Voice Coaches Pro. I decided on a two column system which was the exact reverse of voice coaches.com. The only difference being that more emphasis was put on the main content by shortening the sidebar. The font choice was exactly the same as the original. The design I completed only got as far as the home page as the client needed time to develop the content of the site, although it was a great start to the project and the client was happy with the result.

This site will be fleshed out some day but it probably won’t be finished until much later.
