Voice Coaches Student Access Page Design

The project was completed for Voice Coaches, a client of Overit Media. The purpose of the site was to be a portal for clients to log onto and access all of their online resources while training to be a voice actor from home.

The design was to be modeled off of the flash training courses with a top tab navigation. The design was to work like a web application so it was designed to feel this way. The design was to incorporate an archive of narrative scripts for clients to use as well as a messaging system for interaction between the client and the instructor.

The design was finalized and put into use and is currently being used today by Voice Coaches clientele. I am happy with the design and so is the client.
