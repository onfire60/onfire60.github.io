Available Services

If your interested in hiring me for work please check the relative options that your interested in below.



Web Solutions
  • More Service Info
    Website design

    Prior to the design being created a meeting will be set up to gather information to help purpose the site design to best suit the client’s needs. The web design process includes the creation of wireframes (general layout mockups) of the client’s site design. After wireframes are completed and signed off on, the design will then be converted to the actual graphic representation of client’s site in a general home page and secondary page format (subject to change based on client needs). After these mockups are signed off, further detailed pages will be completed to client’s spec based off the original meeting’s gathered information.

  • More Service Info
    Website maintenance

    Client’s website will be maintained for the terms of the contract. Cost of changes will be based on the individual change, when necessary, whether it is back end, front end, or even design changes. Changes will be taken care of in a timely manner so client will not have to wait months to make minor changes. Changes will be handled in the order in which they arrive so a time range will be given based on when the changes will be completed.

  • More Service Info
    Interactive website design

    Interactive website design entails any design that is either application based or has a user interface. A meeting will take place to go over all functions of the design so that the application will function as intended. After meeting with the client, a basic wireframe blueprint will be created and signed off on before the actual design takes shape.

  • More Service Info
    Web interface & navigation

    Web interface & navigation is just basic navigation for your site. Designs will be incorporated into the client’s existing design. Initially a meeting will take place to discuss how the navigation will function. After meeting, designs will be wire framed based on the current site and this then signed off on. After sign off, the design will be fleshed out.

  • More Service Info
    XHTML/CSS compliant websites

    XHTML/CSS compliant websites means that all sites developed by Onfiredesigns will use all current web techniques and will uphold all standards of the web so the site will be compatible across all browsers to give the client’s users the best experience possible.

  • More Service Info
    Flash websites

    Flash websites entail sites that use adobe flash and take up the full browser real estate. Flash has become an incredible and invaluable tool in web development. It is used to build media players, normal web development, or applications. Flash can even be used to build sites that resemble normal websites with new text layout abilities and can even perform things normal websites can't. This process will go over all aspects of the client’s intended site with an initial meeting. The site will then be wire framed and signed off on. Based on the site’s construction, individual pages will be designed. Development of these sites tends to take a little longer than average sites, but the outcome can be more relevant for the client.

  • More Service Info
    Flash animation / intros

    Adobe Flash can not only be used to build websites but to build interactive animations or site introductions for clients. These can be placed anywhere in a website to give the site a bit of flair and intrigue. Animations will be story boarded on paper and signed off on. The animation will go through a few steps including a basic animation made with less detail to make sure the animation is going in the correct direction. After the basic animation is completed and signed off on, the animation will go through a major overhaul to add the details left out of the first pass. This pass will be reviewed and any minor changes will take place in the final pass.

  • More Service Info
    Banner ads

    Banner ads can either be done in flash or in a static image design. These can be used to advertise elements of the client’s company or to bring people to a website. Either way banner ads are a good way to promote on outer sites that are relevant to your company.

  • More Service Info
    Flash video

    Flash video entails building a video player using adobe flash that is geared and designed based on the client’s current site. Designs will be wire framed and signed off on; then full design for the player will be created and developed for the client. Videos can be formatted for the player as well, and payer can be worked into a content management system for ease of updating.

  • More Service Info
    Dynamic database driven websites

    Dynamic database driven websites will be either built on top of a preexisting content management system like Wordpress or be built into a simple custom content management system so the client will have the ease to make site updates on his/her own. User support for the content management systems will be provided with the site before launch.

  • More Service Info
    Content Management Systems

    Content Management Systems can be used to make any site dynamic and easily updated. The CMS can either be a pre existing CMS like Wordpress or a custom CMS can be developed based on the client’s needs. Building a CMS can take quite a while and can be quite expensive but can result in an experience tailored to the client without any unnecessary overhead.

  • More Service Info
    Blog solutions

    Wordpress is considered to be the number one blogging system available at this time. Blogs can be designed and developed into the client’s existing site. Wordpress can be expanded as a blog exponentially because of its wide user base willing to help you make the most of your blog.

  • More Service Info
    Forum Solutions

    Forum Solutions can be built using one of the best forum systems available Vbulletin. Forum can be designed and developed to fit the user’s current design. Although Vbulletin is not free, it is well worth the expense for any forum user and is well known around the net. User support will be available before the forum is launched.

  • More Service Info
    Web Applications

    Web Applications can be developed in adobe flex or php to help perform tasks that a client requires on the internet. These will perform like real applications installed on any computer. These can be used by the client specifically or can be geared towards the client’s users. Web apps will be blueprinted to get an understanding of the applications main functions and then wire framed to get a view of how the application will be laid out. These steps will individually need to be signed off on before the design is brought to life. The design will be completed and signed off on as well. After the design is completed the development is undertaken and user testing will commence throughout this process. When all is said and done this web app will help the client perform tasks not easily performed when not on the internet.

  • More Service Info
    Html Emails

    Html Emails can be a great way to represent a client’s company through email. Not only does it provide text information but it can also provide a strong visual representation for the client. The designs are generally simple so the information promotes itself successfully.

Print & Presentation Solutions
  • More Service Info
    Logo Identity design

    Creating an identity for any client is very important. It gives the public an official way to recognize the client’s business. Although having an icon or symbol is not always important, sometimes a unique type of treatment can even serve the client just as well if not better. A meeting will be held to go over the ins and outs of the client’s business so the identity fits to a "T". Multiple grey scale design mockup options will be given to the client to choose from. The mockups are given in grey scale because it’s easier to make a design decision when color isn't a distracting factor. When the client chooses one of the options, that logo will be refined and color will be added if necessary.

  • More Service Info
    Business stationery

    Business stationery is designed based on the client’s logo or identity so that when the business sends out any of its printed communications the company can be easily recognized. Designed print stationary can give any client’s communications a more professional look and feel.

  • More Service Info
    Business cards

    Business card designs are based on the client’s logo or identity. Business cards are an invaluable tool for any client who wants to have the ability to leave his/her information and mark behind. Business cards can be very important in business, and what is more important is that the client stands out from the crowd with a uniquely designed business card.

  • More Service Info
    Branded products

    If you are a client that works in the creation of products for its own clients, having a uniquely designed presentation for your product or even the product designed itself can make you stand out from the competition so your business excels. Designs are based on the product itself. Meetings will be held so the product is totally understood before the design is started. General mockups will be made so the client can choose which fits best for their product. After the mockup is chosen and signed off on, the final design will be completed.

  • More Service Info
    Mailers, Fliers & Ads

    Mailers are used to get out information about a company's promotions or other important information. Mailers are old fashioned but still very important as another venue to get your brand recognized. Although mailers can be important, it is also very important to make it unique because generally you will only have a few seconds to catch the recipient’s attention. A meeting will take place to gather the information necessary for the mailer’s creation. Next design mockups will be made for the client to choose from. After the mock up is signed off on, the design will be finalized to be printed.

  • More Service Info
    Icons

    Icons are one way to bring visual interest or even attention to a certain part of a site or printed material. Icons can range from very simple to very detailed but always get a message across. Icons can be designed to be scaled to any size. A meeting will take place to discuss the style and purpose of the icon(s). Hand sketches will be made and signed off on. After sketches are decided upon, general mockups will be made. When accepted, the design will then be finalized and sized based on use.

  • More Service Info
    Powerpoint presentations and slideshows

    PowerPoint presentations can be designed and branded to enhance any presentation. Designs will be based on client’s identity or specific styling.
