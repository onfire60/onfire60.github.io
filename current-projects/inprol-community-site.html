Project

  • INPROL Community Site

    Non-profit Community Based Site
    Date Posted: October 15th, 2010

    This is a new project I'm starting for my current job at Informatics Studio. The work is being done for a non-profit organization called INPROL (International Network to Promote the Rule of Law). The purpose of this site is to revamp a current site with new and updated social technology available across the net. The current site exists at http://www.inprol.org/visitorhome. As you can see the sites design and construction feels a bit dated. It will be our job at Informatics to take this site to the next level.

    My role in this project will be to take care of the development end of the site. I will be developing this site in Drupal 6 mainly because Drupal 7 isnt currently ready for production sites yet. This site will consist of many parts which will include as of the time of writing this post:


    1. Basic information pages
    2. Huge topic taxonomy to classify posts under
    3. Country taxonomy to classify the posts even furthur
    4. Quotes
    5. Pulling in twitter tweets
    6. Community (Organic Groups)
    7. Private messaging
    8. Friend tagging
    9. Subscriptions to users posts (Following Users)
    10. News and features posts
    11. Discussion Forums
    12. Jobs
    13. Events w/ Calendars
    14. Term Library

    I'm sure there will be much more to tell about this project in the future. At this time i have the basic drupal install finished as well as basic module installs and configurations completed. I will keep this page updated when time allows. Until then busy, busy, busy!
